Frequently Asked Questions

When does registration close?

August 25th


What is the address of the retreat?

27425 Spruce Ln

Evergreen, CO 80439


How many spots are there?

11 people can attend this retreat


Do you offer a payment plan?

Yes, if you would like to divide your payments you may pay in three installments between when you book and when registration closes.


What about the use of my phone/electronics?

There will be wi-fi however during program activities we ask that phones are left in your room or to the side so you can fully focus on yourself and get the most benefit from your retreat! We absolutely understand that you may need to check in from time to time.


Will there be alcohol provided or allowed?

There will be no alcohol provided or permitted at this retreat but we will have mocktails and other fun drinks to enjoy! We hope this helps with optimal retreat benefits.


Is the building handicapped accessible?

Everything can be done on the main level and upon request we can ensure you do not have a room on the second floor.


What do I need to pack?

Journal and pen (if you don’t have these, we will have extras)

Warm clothing for outside (burning ceremony)

Bathing suit if you want to go in the jacuzzi

Yoga mat if you have one, we’ll have extras

Meals, snacks and drinks are provided, but you are welcome to bring any special foods you like to have on hand

Hiking gear (optional if you want to join in on a group hike afterwards)

All linens are provided

Earplugs and eye masks if you need them

Your regular toiletries

Medications
