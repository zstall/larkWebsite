Challenging the Inner Critic

A weekend for connection and emotional awareness.


September 8th-10th in Evergreen, Colorado

Join us for an all inclusive licensed therapist led weekend retreat focused on individual and group work to reduce your anxiety for the weekend and hopefully for months to come. While there will be therapeutic work there will also be yoga, meditation, jacuzzi and fun to allow for balance. This all inclusive retreat allows you to put away the worries of cooking or planning and focus on you!

Mindfulness

Anxiety and Stress Reduction

Innovative and creative ideas for dealing with anxiety and stress

Group connection and thoughtful discussion and one individual session with a therapist for each participant


Expertise

Psychiatric nurse practitioner group on supplements, medications and the latest on ketamine


Relaxation

Yoga, Guided meditation, Time for reflection and journaling, Private jacuzzi on property, Reiki session (additional cost), and Tarot card readings (optional)


Wonderful Amenities
