Welcome to Evergreen

This retreat will be held in a beautiful and unique bed and breakfast turned Airbnb that we will have completely to ourselves. With two stories, each of the 11 rooms have unique and fun themes with private bathrooms for everyone. Your hosts will stay on the property in a detached cabin.

The property boasts a beautiful large deck perfect for reflection, yoga, relaxation or a nap. In the back is a private jacuzzi and plenty of space for people to spread out or come together!

Weather dependent, we will either gather in the living space or outside for groups.

All in Evergreen, CO just 45 minutes away from Denver.
